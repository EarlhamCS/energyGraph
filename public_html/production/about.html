About

The energy eDisplay is a project between Earlham College's Hardware Interfacing Project, Green Science Group, and the Maintenance Department. Magnets around each buildings' main power line collect and store values in a database which we harvest and visualize here using Google's Chart and Visualization API.

Normalization is the practice of performing transformations on data in a way that highlights the important parts. Normalized electrical energy values account for the differing populations and architectural features of the buildings. The noramlization algorithm is ` normalized_value = raw_value / (occupancy * building_envelope) ' where building_envelope is a number that models the heat loss of the building. For example, a building with small surface area and good insulation would have a low building number. We also offer an unnormalized view.
